# Borders & shadows

## Borders

`border` (default 1px), `border-strong`, `border-primary`, `border-0`, `border-t`, `border-b`.

## Radius

`rounded-none`, `rounded-xs`, `rounded-sm`, `rounded`, `rounded-md`, `rounded-lg`, `rounded-xl`, `rounded-2xl`, `rounded-pill`, `rounded-full`.

## Shadow

`shadow-none`, `shadow-xs`, `shadow-sm`, `shadow`, `shadow-lg`, `shadow-xl`, `shadow-glow`.
